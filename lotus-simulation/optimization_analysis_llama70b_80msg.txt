============================================================
C) Why optimization helps some parts but not all
============================================================
1) Workflow structure
- mem0/evermemos rely on sem_filter checkpoints where helper cascade cleanly routes many decisions.
- zep is join-dominant; sem_join complexity grows with memory size and is more sensitive to cascade planning and data-shape edge cases.

2) LOTUS operator behavior
- sem_filter cascade (with helper proxy) is stable in these runs.
- sem_join cascade is active but can encounter fallback in certain index/data patterns (seen repeatedly in zep Q5).

3) Data-growth effects at 80 messages
- Larger history increases candidate pairs and join/search workload.
- evermemos creates more boundaries/segments with longer conversation horizon.
- zep accumulates larger graph state, amplifying join passes and fallback exposure.
